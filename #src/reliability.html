Time to market
According to Forbes, 90% of startups fail. There are various reasons, one of which is market incompliance at the time of release. The solution? Speed up your development by means of load and chaos testing. Our engineers work closely with clients' development teams to ensure that their application can withstand instant load spikes.
No downtime
As we previously mentioned, in 2020 more and more products are being developed as web services for which uptime is a crucial aspect for growth. To avoid unpredicted downtime we automate backup systems and ensure that the system starts an identical environment in which the service is set up when the system goes down.
Fault-tolerance
A resilient and fault-tolerant infrastructure continues functioning and providing service even after a part of it has suffered a failure. This buys teams some time to find out what happened and fix the issue. Our uptime optimization services result in a win-win situation: clients can use the product and the company profits.
